All About Copa America 2021

Champion

Argentina won their first major title in 28 years on Saturday when an Angel Di Maria goal gave them a 1-0 win over Brazil and a record-equalling 15th Copa America.

Runners-up

Argentina win the Copa America title for the first time since 1993 as they beat Brazil 1-0 in the final at the historic Maracana Stadium in Rio de Janeiro.

Player Of The Tournament

Lionel Messi was named best player of the oldest national team tournament in the world. His goals, assists and leadership drove Argentina forward to win the title.
